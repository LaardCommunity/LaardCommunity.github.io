The second in-person LAARD community meeting will take place on the first day of [UKAS 2026](https://www.ukas2026.co.uk/)
(30th March) in Bristol. The event will run from 2–6 pm, finishing just in time for the UKAS keynote lecture by Prof.
Richard Evershed.

This will be a great opportunity to network with fellow lipid enthusiasts at the start of a busy conference and to get
to know some friendly faces over drinks and nibbles. We will also be organising laboratory tours of the School of
Chemistry for those interested!

| When? | Where? | What? |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 2–6 pm, Monday 30th March 2026 | [School of Chemistry, University of Bristol](https://maps.app.goo.gl/qpX3Lg9mAqjjEe2J6) | Informal meeting with drinks & nibbles |
