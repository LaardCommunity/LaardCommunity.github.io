We're delighted to announce that our newly elected Steering Committee members have been successfully installed! We hope
you'll join us in thanking the retiring members for their service, and wishing new members the very best of luck.

Mélanie Roffet-Salque has stepped down, with Léa Drieu taking over as Chair. New member Jasmine Lundy replaces Léa as
Secretary. Nàdia Tarifa Mateo (Engagement team) and Nick Hall (Webmaster/Communications team) take over from Shinya
Shoda and Adrià Breu, respectively. Karine Taché (vice-Chair/Treasurer) and Julia Becher (Engagement team) remain in
their existing posts.
