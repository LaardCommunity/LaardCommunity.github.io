The second in-person LAARD community meeting will take place on the first day of [UKAS 2026](https://www.ukas2026.co.uk/)
(30th March) in Bristol. The event will run from 2–6 pm, finishing just in time for the UKAS keynote lecture by Prof.
Richard Evershed. This will be a great opportunity to network with fellow lipid enthusiasts at the start of a busy
conference and to get to know some friendly faces over drinks and nibbles. We will also be organising laboratory tours
of the School of Chemistry for those interested! Do register for the LAARD event using the button above, and follow
updates from the UKAS team over on the conference website.
